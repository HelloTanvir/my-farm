How it works
1. Fund Your Account
Use our website or download our app to create your My Farm account and transfer funds there to purchase available agricultural assets.
2. Purchase Your Assets
After the fund transfer in your account you will receive list of available agricultural assets and you can purchase with just a click.
3. Watch Them Grow
Log into your My Farm account and watch your farm mature and grow. Weekly data will be available along with current health status, value of your asset, and profit projection on your dashboard.
4. Receive Your Returns
After the maturation period of your asset, it is sold and the return and investment are deposited in you My Farm account. You can keep, withdraw or make an investment again through your My Farm account.
